If you need logic-heavy forms, payments, or workflow integrations, these 20 platforms offer powerful features on free tiers — pick based on developer needs, integrations, or self-hosting options.

1. [Typeform](https://www.typeform.com/) — highly engaging, conversational forms with rich branching and scoring.
2. [Jotform](https://www.jotform.com/) — versatile form builder with approvals, conditional logic, and document generation.
3. [Tally](https://tally.so/) — minimalist UI with surprisingly deep logic, hidden fields, and automation hooks.
4. [Paperform](https://paperform.co/) — forms that double as landing pages and support advanced conditional rules.
5. [Cognito Forms](https://www.cognitoforms.com/) — free tier with repeating sections, calculations, and workflow rules.
6. [Form.io](https://form.io/) — API-first forms platform for developers: JSON schema, APIs, and complex validation.
7. [Formstack](https://www.formstack.com/) — enterprise workflows, integrations, and secure data handling.
8. [Zoho Forms](https://www.zoho.com/forms/) — powerful rules and approval flows that tie into the Zoho ecosystem.
9. [Microsoft Forms](https://forms.microsoft.com/) — quick surveys with branching that integrate deeply with Microsoft 365.
10. [SurveyMonkey](https://www.surveymonkey.com/) — survey-first tool with advanced skip logic and analytics.
11. [HubSpot Forms](https://www.hubspot.com/products/forms) — CRM-connected forms with automation triggers and smart fields.
12. [Formcarry](https://formcarry.com/) — simple form endpoints with advanced validations for developers.
13. [Wufoo](https://www.wufoo.com/) — mature form builder with payments, logic, and reporting.
14. [Formsite](https://www.formsite.com/) — nested logic, calculations, and secure workflows for complex surveys.
15. [OhMyForm](https://ohmyform.com/) — open-source and self-hostable alternative to Typeform with deep customization.
16. [Fabrica Forms (WordPress)](https://wordpress.org/plugins/fabrica-forms/) — WP plugin for conditional visibility and calculated fields.
17. [Gravity Forms](https://www.gravityforms.com/) — powerful WordPress ecosystem with advanced logic and many add-ons.
18. [LimeSurvey](https://www.limesurvey.org/) — open-source survey platform designed for complex question routing.
19. [Qualtrics](https://www.qualtrics.com/) — research-grade surveys and analytics (free student/basic plans available).
20. [Airtable Forms](https://airtable.com/forms) — database-connected forms that trigger automations and workflows.
